I am currently developing a device that will monitor the environment. It will consist of multiple stations distributed across Poland, which will display the results on a map. The device will monitor various aspects, including natural radiation using a scintillation counter, air quality using a PM2.5 sensor, electromagnetic pulses using a custom-made sensor, and UV index.
